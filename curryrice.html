Curry Rice

Prep time

15mins

Cook time

30mins

Total time

45mins

Equipment

  • Glass mixing bowl
  • Wooden spatula
  • Whisk

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1tsp vanilla
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g melted butter
  • 50ml pancake syrup

Instructions

  1. Mix all dry Ingredients in the mixing bowl
  2. In a separate bowl mix milk, eggs,butter and Vanilla
  3. Pour wet Ingredients in dry Ingredients
  4. Whisk together to form a thick paste
  5. In a frying pan pour paste in bits
  6. Fry both sides until they turn golden brown
  7. Serve in plate and dig in
